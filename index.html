November, 2024

PowerBI PROJECT

The e-commerce sales analysis reveals a total profit of ₹37K with overall sales amounting to ₹438K and 5,615 units sold. Clothing and Electronics emerged as the most profitable categories, contributing 36.05% and 35.61% respectively. Among payment methods, Cash on Delivery (COD) was the most preferred, accounting for 35.45% of the transactions. The top five customers include Madan Mohan, Aarushi, and Shrichand, who contributed significantly to sales. Madhya Pradesh and Maharashtra were the leading states in terms of revenue, with Madhya Pradesh alone contributing 31.67%. Profitability was highest in February, March, and November, while losses were observed during the months of May to September and again in December. This analysis highlights the importance of targeting high-performing categories, customers, and regions while addressing mid-year performance dips.
